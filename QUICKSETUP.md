## 🖥️ OPTION A — View Locally (No Internet Hosting Needed)

**Takes: 10 seconds**

1. Open the `ivette-media-website` folder on your computer
2. Double-click **`index.html`**
3. It opens in your browser — done! ✅

> Navigate between pages using the menu links just like a live website.

---

## 🌐 OPTION B — Go Live Free with Netlify (Recommended)

**Takes: 2 minutes — no account setup needed**

1. Go to **[netlify.com/drop](https://app.netlify.com/drop)**
2. Drag and drop your entire `ivette-media-website` folder onto the page
3. Wait ~10 seconds for it to upload
4. Netlify gives you a free live URL instantly (e.g. `random-name.netlify.app`)
5. Optional: click **"Set up a custom domain"** to use your own domain name (e.g. `ivettemedia.com`)

> 💡 **Tip:** Create a free Netlify account to keep control of your site and update it later.

---

## 🐙 OPTION C — GitHub Pages (Free & Version Controlled)

**Takes: 5 minutes**

1. Create a free account at **[github.com](https://github.com)**
2. Click **"New repository"** — name it `ivettemedia-website`
3. Upload all 7 files to the repository
4. Go to **Settings → Pages**
5. Under "Source", select **"Deploy from branch"** → choose `main` → click **Save**
6. Your site will be live at `yourusername.github.io/ivettemedia-website` ✅

---

## ▲ OPTION D — Vercel (Fast & Professional)

**Takes: 3 minutes**

1. Go to **[vercel.com](https://vercel.com)** and sign up free
2. Click **"Add New → Project"**
3. Upload your folder or connect your GitHub repo (if using Option C)
4. Click **Deploy** — Vercel handles everything automatically
5. Get your live URL instantly ✅

## 🌍 Adding a Custom Domain Name

If you have a domain (e.g. `ivettemedia.com`):

1. Log into your domain registrar (GoDaddy, Namecheap, etc.)
2. Go to **DNS Settings**
3. Point your domain to your hosting platform:
   - **Netlify:** Follow [netlify.com/docs/custom-domains](https://docs.netlify.com/domains-https/custom-domains/)
   - **Vercel:** Follow [vercel.com/docs/concepts/projects/domains](https://vercel.com/docs/concepts/projects/domains)
   - **GitHub Pages:** Follow [docs.github.com/pages/configuring-a-custom-domain](https://docs.github.com/en/pages/configuring-a-custom-domain-for-your-github-pages-site)

## ❓ Troubleshooting

| Problem | Fix |
|---------|-----|
| Logo not showing | Make sure `logo.jpg` is in the **same folder** as the HTML files |
| Fonts look wrong | You need an internet connection for Google Fonts to load |
| Dark mode not saving | Make sure your browser allows `localStorage` (disable strict privacy mode) |
| Page links broken | All 4 HTML files must be in the **same folder** |
| Site looks broken on mobile | Check you haven't accidentally deleted any `</div>` or `</section>` tags |
